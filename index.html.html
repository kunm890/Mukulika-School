Nursery — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Socialization, oral language games, motor skills, colors & shapes.
Term 2: Phonemic awareness, pre-writing activities, number sense (1–10), nature walks.
Term 3: Storytelling, songs, seasonal projects, show-and-tell, readiness for LKG.

L.K.G — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Alphabet recognition, counting to 20, fine motor skill practice.
Term 2: Simple words and sight-words, basic addition & subtraction, environmental awareness.
Term 3: Reading short sentences, handwriting formation, arts & craft themes.

U.K.G — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Fluent letter sounds, number bonds to 20, oral fluency sessions.
Term 2: Basic grammar (nouns/verbs), problem solving, simple science experiments.
Term 3: Reading comprehension starters, project work, readiness for Class 1.

Class 1 — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Phonics, number sense to 100, environmental studies basics.
Term 2: Simple sentence construction, addition & subtraction strategies, art integration.
Term 3: Story comprehension, measurement & geometry basics, project presentation.

Class 2 — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Reading fluency, place value, local geography & science observations.
Term 2: Paragraph writing, multiplication introduction, plant & animal studies.
Term 3: Creative writing, fraction basics, simple experiments & field trips.

Class 3 — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Reading comprehension, multiplication & division, water cycle & environment.
Term 2: Grammar building, measurement, local history & Civics introduction.
Term 3: Creative projects, basic data handling, science projects.

Class 4 — Yearly Lesson Plan (Overview)

Term 1: Advanced comprehension, long division, energy & resources topics.
Term 2: Paragraph & essay writing, fractions & decimals, ecosystem studies.
Term 3: Project-based learning, basic map skills, exam preparation and revision.
